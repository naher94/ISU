Hierarchy

We have a large general body that meets once a semester, a general board that meets every week, and an executive board that leads those meetings.

About

We are a multicultural undergraduate student organization whose purpose is to promote the interaction of any nature between all members of the university, with the overarching purpose to spread progressive awareness of cultures from around the world.

ISU was started as a way to combine the energies of the different cultures on campus and to work together to do booth at Carnival every year, growing into a tight-knit community of friends. From these friends came ISU as we see it today moving to focus on being involved on campus throughout the year.

Smoothies

This Spring we ran a smoothie fundraiser as a way to bring in the great weather and let everyone know about the new ISU and what there is to come.

Hot Chocolate Sale

Our hot chocolate sale was a way we could warm people up during the winter as well as inform people about how people from around the world make and enjoy their hot chocolate. From mint, cinnamon and chili peppers.

International Food Festival

Our food festival brings together great people and great food that many people would not have the opportunity to enjoy. Getting as many participants who personally grew up enjoy some part of their culture and sharing it with the rest of the community.

Wavin' Flags

Wavin' Flags is a late night event run each year towards the end of the fall semester as a way to end the year and bring together many of the international organizations on campus, sharing foods, culture, games, dance, and music to name a few.
